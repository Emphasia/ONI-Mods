[h1][b]A piped LiquidBottler.[/b][/h1]
uses CanisterFiller's animation.
[h1]description:[/h1]
[list]
    [*]Allow Duplicants to fetch bottled liquids from pipe for delivery to buildings (like Pitcher-Pump).
    [*]research [b]Improved-Plumbing[/b] must be finished to unlock this building.
    [*]Optional [b]Auto-Drop when Full[/b] (and then continue filling as long as piped).
    [*][b]won't[/b] produce polluted-oxygen when connected to polluted-water (like Liquid-Reservoir).
[/list]
